## AI Research Assistant

An autonomous AI-powered research assistant built with **CrewAI** and **Large Language Models (LLMs)**.

This application uses multiple AI agents that collaborate together to research topics, analyze information, and generate structured reports automatically.

---

##  What is AI Research Assistant?

AI Research Assistant is a multi-agent system designed to automate the research process.

Instead of manually searching, collecting, and summarizing information, the system uses specialized AI agents that work together:

-  Research Agent → Finds and collects information
-  Analysis Agent → Analyzes and organizes the collected data
-  Writer Agent → Creates a final structured report

---

##  Why use this application?

Research tasks usually require:

- Searching multiple sources
- Reading large amounts of information
- Extracting important points
- Writing summaries and reports

This AI assistant reduces the time required by delegating these tasks to autonomous AI agents.
